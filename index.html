Key Innovations

Re-engineering human behavior.

Every feature of SPORE is designed to push digital interaction back toward the physical world — not away from it.

Mass Bloom Events

At concerts, book launches, film premieres, and live events, all attendees simultaneously unlock content through verified group interaction. Each person must tap another's device within the bloom radius — not just the host beacon. Collective presence creates collective access. The crowd becomes the key.

Organic Bond Decay

Connections on SPORE Social aren't permanent by default. A mutually agreed Resonance Frequency (weekly, monthly, quarterly) keeps bonds alive. Miss it, and the connection dims — until you meet again in person.

Proximity-Earned Communication

Digital messaging between any two SPORES is gated by physical time spent together. Credits are earned in person. When they deplete, the only available action is proposing a real-world meeting.

Source Resonance Badge

Every piece of content displays a verifiable badge: Source Spore (original) or Echo Spore (shared). The distinction is enforced by an immutable on-chain smart contract — never gameable.

Shared Full View in Proximity

When two verified SPORES are in close proximity, full profiles and content become visible to each other. The moment proximity breaks, content returns to preview — designed to drive real reconnection.

Trust isn't assumed. It's proven.
